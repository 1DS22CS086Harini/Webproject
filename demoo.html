About Us

Anmol is a restaurant designed around the farmers haul,featuring a menu that changes daily with a focus on seasonal and local cuisine. Heirloom grains are milled fresh for house made pastas,breads and desserts,whole animal butchery is done on site, and an extensive in house fermentation program captures flavours at their peak and preserves them for the off season.
